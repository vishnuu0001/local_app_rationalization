## Slide 4 — What the Platform Delivers

### Five Capabilities That Drive Business Value

**1. Executive Portfolio Dashboard**
A real-time, single-screen view of your entire application landscape. Total application count, health scores, retirement candidates, and investment priorities — visible to leadership in seconds, not weeks.

**2. Objective Application Health Scoring**
Quantifies technical debt, code complexity, and security exposure for every application using industry-standard analysis. Every score is objective, defensible, and auditable — no analyst bias.

**3. Business Capability Mapping**
Maps every application to the business capabilities it supports. Instantly surfaces redundancy (multiple apps doing the same thing) and gaps (business capabilities with no adequate application support).

**4. Industry Benchmarking**
Compares your portfolio health and rationalization maturity against 195+ industry benchmarks. Gives leadership objective, external validation for every investment decision.

**5. Rationalization Recommendations — The 4R Framework**
Every application is automatically scored against four strategic options:

| Decision | Meaning | Business Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| **Retire** | Decommission — no longer needed | Immediate cost elimination |
| **Replace** | Swap for a standard or SaaS alternative | Lower TCO, modern capability |
| **Re-platform** | Migrate to cloud | Agility, scalability, cost optimization |
| **Retain** | Keep and invest in modernization | Protect strategic value |
